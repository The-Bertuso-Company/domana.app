Domana (“we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details: Name, email address, phone number (if you create an account or list a property)
  • Property information: Listing details, photos, location
  • Usage data: Device type, app version, pages visited, and interactions
  • Location data (optional): If you enable location-based search

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Displaying and managing property listings
  • Communicating with you about your account or listings
  • Improving and securing our services
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Data is stored securely with access controls and encryption where applicable. We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

  • With service providers who help us operate our platform
  • When required by law or government regulation
  • With your consent

5. Your Rights (Philippines Data Privacy Act)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, withdraw consent, and lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission.
